St George College Aruvithura

ABOUT

St. George’s College, Aruvithura (Kottayam, Kerala), affiliated with MG University and founded in 1965, offers diverse undergraduate, postgraduate, and vocational programs in arts, science, and commerce, including BCA, B.Sc, B.Com, M.Sc, MA, and B.Voc in Food Processing. The college is now transitioning to a 4-year undergraduate programme (FYUGP).
